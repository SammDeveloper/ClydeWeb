Easy and secure

Clyde Music was initially created to be easy to use in small servers, but if you are the admin of a larger server you can keep the trolls at bay by restricting who can modify the queue.

Integrations

Clyde Music can play music from YouTube, Spotify, Soundcloud, direct links. Yes, it also supports playlists and livestreams!

Search

Instead of linking directly to a track, you can also choose to simply just type in the name of your song and let Clyde Music find it for you.

Shuffle and Repeat

Clyde Music has full support for shuffling your playlist. You can even choose to put just one or the entire queue on repeat.
